Cherry Grog

Get the night started off right with a sweet, tart, spiced drink.

Ingredients

  • 1 jigger lime juice
  • 2 jiggers tart cherry juice
  • 3 jiggers rum
  • 4 jiggers ginger beer
  • Lime slices and/or cherries, for garnish

Directions

  1. Fill two cocktail glasses halfway with ice.
  2. Combine all ingredients except the garnish in a cocktail mixer or pitcher.
  3. Stir to mix.
  4. Pour equal parts into each glass.
  5. Garnish with lime and/or cherries.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
