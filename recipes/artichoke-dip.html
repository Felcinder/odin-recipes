Artichoke Dip

Take a break from your daily chores with this delicious Artichoke Dip from Stardew Valley!

Ingredients

  • 35 grams scallions
  • 10 grams minced garlic
  • 370 grams artichoke hearts roughly chopped
  • 43 grams butter
  • 430 grams cream cheese room temperature
  • 40 grams parmesan cheese shredded
  • 90 grams gontina cheese shredded
  • pepper
  • nutmeg

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  2. In a pan over medium heat, melt butter.
  3. Add garlic and scallion, heat for 5 minuets.
  4. Add the artichoke hearts and cook for another 5 minuets.
  5. In a bowl, combine the cream cheese, parmesan cheese, and fontina cheese.
  6. Add the artichoke heart and mix well.
  7. Season with nutmeg, salt, and pepper.
  8. Place the dip in a baking dish, bake for 20 minutes.
  9. Turn the broiler on and broil until the top browns.
  10. Serve with chips or baguette slices.
